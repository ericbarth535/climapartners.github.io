What We Do

Clima Capital Partners leverages decades of investment experience to help our clients realize their vision for a greener more sustainable world

We design innovative solutions to climate risks, as well as identifying related business opportunities within specific markets and industries

We spur collaboration among various agents -- Financial Institutions - Public & Private, Private & Institutional Investors, Development Banks, Government Agencies, Project Developers, and Technology Providers -- to create synergies and address complex challenges that our clients face especially in emerging markets

Our Team

Joumana Asso, Managing Director

Joumana has over 18 years of investment and advisory experience in emerging markets, including ten years executing project and corporate finance deals in the heavy industry and equity investments in clean technology start-ups, and eight years in managing investment in climate related assets at the International Finance Corporation (IFC), World Bank, and the Climate Investment Funds (CIF).

Prior to finding Clima, Joumana managed the overall private sector portfolio of the Climate Investment Funds and managed the Clean Technology Fund’s operations in 2009-2011.

Joumana holds an MBA from HEC and a DESS in Environmental Studies from the University of Montreal. She has work experience in China, India, the Balkans, Africa, and the MENA region.

Lawrence McGrath, Managing Director

Twenty years of private equity experience with middle-market buyouts primarily in infrastructure and energy projects either through direct investments and co-investments or by establishing a consortium of like-minded investors. Acquired and developed infrastructure projects in central and western Europe which include the conversion of coal-fired district heating companies to combined heat and power plants and eventually a partial conversion to natural gas. Background includes having worked extensively with western European companies in developing biogas for transportation and power generation, solar, wind and run of the river hydro projects.

My career began with the acquisitions of State-owned electric distribution and power companies in Peru, Argentina, Brazil and Bolivia during the 1990’s. As a result, I am familiar with contracted power generation projects and regulated utilities.

Responsibilities included origination, shareholder agreement negotiations, deal structuring, due diligence, financial analysis, strategic sales and fund management. Member of the investment committee and Board representative for the companies acquired under my sponsorship.

Fernando Rivas

Mr. Rivas has twenty years of extensive energy transactions experience in Latin America. At FE Clean Energy, Mr. Rivas identifies projects and develops relationships in Latin America and in Spain, helping him to build the current project pipeline for investments in Latin America and certain European countries. His primary responsibilities are centered on project origination, structuring, evaluation and due diligence in Latin America. Prior to joining FE Clean Energy, Mr. Rivas was Latin American Business Developer for Petrobras Energia responsible for project development in South America and Mexico. Before that, Mr. Rivas was part of the team that managed one of the earliest private equity funds in Argentina, which invested in small family companies in both Argentina and Brazil.

Mr. Rivas graduated from the Belgrano University (Licenciado en Administracion, Argentina) and holds an M.B.A. in Finance from International Business School of Buenos Aires. He is a native Spanish speaker and is fluent in English and Portuguese.

Rachel Stern

Sohee Gu
